# Which niche dimensions?

- **Grinnellian niche** concept (habitat, tolerance to abiotic env.)
  - Temperature, moisture, pH, salinity, â€¦ spatial and temporal dim.
- **Life history** strategies (demographic response to env. variation)
  - Reproductive mode, CSR, EPO, LHS models
- **Nutritional/trophic** dimension
  - Feeding mechanisms, foraging strategies
- **Survival/defense**
  - Avoidance, resistance
- **Physiological/metabolic**
  - slow/fast metabolism; acquisition/conservation
